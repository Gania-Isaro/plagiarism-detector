Rwanda is a beautiful country in East Africa, known for its rolling hills, lakes, and forests. 
The capital city, Kigali, is modern and safe, attracting both visitors and investors. 
The country has made remarkable progress since the 1994 genocide, emphasizing peace, reconciliation, and economic growth.

Tourists visit Rwanda to see the mountain gorillas in Volcanoes National Park and to enjoy its scenic landscapes. 
Agriculture is the backbone of the economy, especially coffee and tea exports. 
The people are friendly and hardworking, and the government promotes education, technology, and environmental conservation. 
Rwanda combines natural beauty, rich culture, and development, making it a unique nation in Africa.
