Rwanda is famous for its beautiful landscapes and green hills. Tourism plays an important role in the economy.  
Many people visit to see the mountain gorillas in Volcanoes National Park. Other popular places include Lake Kivu and Nyungwe Forest.  
Tourism helps local communities by creating jobs and supporting eco-tourism projects.  
The government encourages visitors to travel safely and explore Rwanda’s natural beauty.  
Tourists also visit Kigali for cultural experiences and to learn about Rwanda’s history.
