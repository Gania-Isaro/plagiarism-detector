Rwanda is a small country in East Africa, often called the Land of a Thousand Hills because of its beautiful landscapes. 
Its capital, Kigali, is clean, safe, and modern. The country is famous for mountain gorillas in Volcanoes National Park, which attract many tourists each year.
Agriculture is very important in Rwanda, with coffee and tea being the main exports. 
The nation has recovered from the 1994 genocide and now focuses on peace, unity, and development. 
Education and technology are growing sectors, helping young people gain new skills. 
Rwandans are known for their hospitality, hard work, and commitment to progress while preserving nature and culture.
