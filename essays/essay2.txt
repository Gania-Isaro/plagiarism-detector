Rwanda is known for its rolling green hills and scenic landscapes. Tourism is an important part of the economy.  
Many tourists come to see mountain gorillas in Volcanoes National Park. Other attractions include Nyungwe Forest and Lake Kivu.  
Eco-tourism programs help local communities and provide employment opportunities.  
The government promotes safe travel and encourages visitors to enjoy Rwanda’s natural beauty.  
Visitors also go to Kigali to experience the culture and learn about the country’s history.
